St. Croix Bike and Pedestrian Trails Coalition (SCBPTC)

StCroixTrails.com

Being an outdoor enthusiast (including biking and running), I joined the New Richmond Run Club and New Richmond Pathways Committee.
With my interest, I was appointed the City's representative to a County trail group, the St. Croix Bike and Pedestrian Trails Coalition.
The group was welcoming, productive, and I believed in their mission to "...support St. Croix County and local municipalities in the development of a multi-use trail system..."
In September 2014, I was elected to the Coalitions initial Board of Directors, re-elected in April 2017, and voted Vice-Chairperson.
