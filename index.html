Welcome!


My name is Andrew Lamers, I'm a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Professional

Visit the menu to find my portfolio, resume, and contact information.


I was introduced to GIS in 2008, by Dr. Ron Ward, while attending Normandale Community College.

Once my Associate Degree was complete, I transfered to the University of Minnesota where I would major in GIS while pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree.
In May 2011, I graduated from the U of M and began working for the City of Eden Prairie.

After working in the public sector, as a GIS Technician and IT/GIS Analyst, I decided to return to school for a Master's Degree in GIS/Cartography.

I currently work at SEH Inc, a private engineering firm, as their GIS Administrator.
